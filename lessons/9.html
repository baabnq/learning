Lession 9 - Naming Conventions

lab MyModule::Routine;
    put _local = 0;
    new 'example' _sLocalString;
    
    lab MyModule::Routine::OuterSublabel;
        lab MyModule::Routine::OuterSublabel::InnerSublabel;
        
    return;

Note that: These conventions are only guidelines and not rigid rules. It's generally a good idea to follow these, but you can to whatever you want, i (probably) won't stop you.

Routines and Sublabels

Labels in routines always follow this format: lab <module name>::<routine name>::<sublabel 0>:: ... ;
