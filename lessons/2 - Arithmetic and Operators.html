Lesson 2 - Arithmetic and Operators

Doing math works exactly as you would expect. So this here will give brief examples for each operator. Important to remember is, though, that Baabnq doesn't have unary operators; it only has binary operators.

Addition and Subtraction

put _a = 2;
put _b = 5;
put _c = _a + _b;
print _c - 3;

Bitwise And

print 3 & 5;

Bitwise Or

print 3 | 5;

Bitwise Xor

print 3 ^ 5;

Shift Right

Shift Right is also referred to as Shift Great because it moves in the direction of the MSb. 

print 1 << 3;

Shift Left

Inversely, Shift Left is also referred to as Shift Small for the same reason. 

print 8 >> 3;
